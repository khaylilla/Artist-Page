Urie Mise

A digital artist specializing in semi realistic-style artworks. Artist Urie Mise masterfully balances contrasting emotions in their artwork, blending melancholic and joyful themes through expressive characters, symbolic elements, and atmospheric storytelling. Their unique style, soft lighting, and attention to detail create visually captivating pieces that evoke deep emotional connections.
